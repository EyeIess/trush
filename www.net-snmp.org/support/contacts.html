Contacting the Authors

The Net-SNMP community consists of many people volunteering their time to improve the software and to provide end-user support. No member of the Net-SNMP team is being paid to provide these services. Please consider this when requesting help.

The recommended method for obtaining support is to use the net-snmp-users or net-snmp-coders mail list. However, before posting your question to either of these lists, please be sure to try the following resources first:

  • README files
  • FAQ
  • Man pages
  • Net-SNMP-Users mail archive
  • Net-SNMP-Coders mail archive
  • Bug tracker
  • Patches tracker

Please read the rules on the mail list page before posting your question on the mail lists.
