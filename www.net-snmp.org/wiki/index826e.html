This is the meeting agenda/notes for the Meeting held on 2009-11-16.

Date and Time: 2009-11-16 20:00 UTC

Agenda

  • Next Meeting Date
    • Dec 14th
  • Releases
    • 5.2.x -- dts12
      • pre1 is out
    • 5.4.x -- dts12
      • pre1 is out
    • notes from Dave:
      • used the older makerelease script
      • needs an easy to install makerelease
  • Coding Issues
    • magfr expressed extreme fustration with the new agent architecture over it's storage of too many OIDs
      • he'll come up with a possible proposal for fixing it
    • TrogL: issues with multiple CPUs and reporting
      • will come up with a table plan and we'll work on it from there; may float requests by -users
  • Testing infrastructure and supported releases
    • Wes' proposal from the last meeting
      • Details still needed
        • What test output?
          • just pass/fail at first to avoid privacy and anonymous data issues at first
        • What O/S info?
          • uname and ./config.guess
        • What net-snmp config?
          • we'd like configure options and HAVE_* stuff, but it's not likely to be easy privacy wise.
      • Wes will try and put something together in Q1 2010
  • Future Directions
    • we had a lot of longer term discussions today (adove) so nothing further was brought up
  • Other Issues
  • Round Table
    • TrogL to work more closely with the OpenSolaris group and will be doing more SNMP work. He'll try to communicate both ways about the Net-SNMP project.
    • Wes and Robert will hopefully be doing more work in the next 6 months.
    • magfr: trying to create a test case for discerning oid.2.26 from oid.2.64.0 for agentx subagent registrations
    • dts12: primarily the 5.2.x and 5.4.x releases.
