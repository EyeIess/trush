This page provides a categorised summary of the outstanding entries in the Patches tracker, which were submitted for consideration, but have not yet been either accepted or rejected.

It does not include entries that have been discussed at one of the patch-related BugBash meetings, and are still under consideration. It does include all patches submitted up to 2008-10-13.
