The guy who started the project... When all else fails, blame him.

I have two careers in life: I'm a photographer trapped inside the body of a computer scientist. I spend all my waking hours generally doing one of those two embodiments and love them both.

On the photography side of things, I concentrate mostly on nature photography and if you're ever in the Davis, CA, US area let me know and I'll give you a tour of where some of my pictures are hanging.

On the computer science side of things, I concentrate mostly on internet security related topics and spend a lot of time working on protocol interoperability via IETF.
