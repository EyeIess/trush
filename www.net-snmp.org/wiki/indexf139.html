Administrative Meetings

From Net-SNMP Wiki
Jump to: navigation, search

The Net-SNMP administrative staff holds meetings on IRC on a regular basis to discuss project issues, goals, etc. The meetings are run on the #net-snmp-admin channel. Anyone can join and participate. To add agenda items, simply add them to the list in the upcoming meetings pages. If you feel the minutes of past meetings are in error, please edit them as appropriate.
